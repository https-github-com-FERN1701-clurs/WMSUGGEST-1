WMSUGGEST

A Course Recommendation System

About WMSUGGEST

WMSUGGEST is a course recommendation system that helps students choose the right courses based on their attributes prior to college, including their WMSU College Entrance Test.

It is a powerful tool that helps students make informed decisions about their college courses. By using the system, students can maximize their learning potential and achieve their academic and career goals.

How it works?

The system requires the student to sign up and complete their profile. The profile will include personal information such as their name, sex, and email, as well as student personality type and senior high school information, such as their SHS GPA and SHS strand or track, including the name of the school where they studied SHS and the WMSU CET.

The system will then suggest a list of courses that are the best match for the student data
